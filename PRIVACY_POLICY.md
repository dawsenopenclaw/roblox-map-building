## OVERVIEW

RobloxForge ("**Company**," "**we**," "**us**," "**our**") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our website, mobile application, and platform (collectively, the "**Service**").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 1.1 Account Data

When you create an account, we collect:
- Username / display name
- Email address
- Password (hashed and salted, never stored in plain text)
- Date of birth (month and year only, not day)
- First and last name (optional)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Timezone (for notifications)

**For parental accounts (users under 13):**
- Parent/guardian email address
- Parental consent verification token (one-time use)
- Parental consent timestamp and IP address

### 1.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the Service:
- Games and assets you create
- Projects you access and modify (dates, durations)
- AI generation requests (prompts, outputs, timestamps)
- Collaboration activities (team memberships, invitations)
- Assets you purchase from the Marketplace
- Features and tools you use

**What we do NOT track:**
- Keystroke-by-keystroke activity
- Individual mouse movements
- Behavioral profiles for targeted advertising
- Your Roblox game play outside RobloxForge

### 1.3 Device and Access Data

- Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
- Operating system and version
- Browser type and version
- IP address (automatically purged after 30 days)
- Approximate geographic location (country/state level, inferred from IP — never GPS)
- Timestamps of login and logout events
- Device identifier (optional, only with your permission)

### 1.4 Payment Data

When you make a purchase, we collect:
- Transaction amount and currency
- Transaction date and time
- Item purchased (token pack, subscription tier, marketplace asset)
- Subscription plan and billing cycle
- Payment method type (credit card brand, last 4 digits only)
- Stripe customer ID (for recurring charges)

**What we do NOT store:**
- Full credit card numbers (Stripe handles this)
- CVV or security codes
- Bank account details (for payouts)

### 1.5 AI Generation Data

When you use RobloxForge's AI features (game generation, asset creation, code suggestions):
- Your text prompts
- Generated game files and assets
- Model version used (e.g., Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Meshy, etc.)
- Generation parameters (style, size, quality settings)
- Whether you approved, rejected, or modified the output
- Time spent on each generation

**For under-13 users:** We do not collect or train on AI generation data without explicit parental consent.

### 1.6 Communications Data

- Support tickets and emails you send us
- Chat messages in team collaborations (stored on our servers)
- Push notifications and email preferences
- Feedback, surveys, and user research responses (optional)

### 1.7 Compliance and Security Data

- Parental consent tokens and verification logs (5+ years retention for FTC audit)
- DMCA takedown notices you submit or receive
- Fraud detection signals (attempt counts, unusual login patterns)
- Account security events (password changes, suspicious access attempts)

## 5. YOUR RIGHTS — GDPR (EU/EEA/UK)

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

### 5.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

**How to exercise:**
- Email legal@robloxforge.app with subject "GDPR Data Access Request"
- Include your name, email, and account ID (if available)
- Response deadline: 15 calendar days

**What you'll receive:**
- Complete data export in machine-readable format (CSV, JSON)
- Documentation of third parties we share data with
- Description of how your data is used

### 5.2 Right to Rectification

You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

**How to exercise:**
- Update profile information directly in your account settings
- Email legal@robloxforge.app for data you cannot update yourself
- Response deadline: 15 calendar days

### 5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data, except where we have a legal obligation to retain it.

**How to exercise:**
- Click "Delete Account" in Account Settings → Delete Account
- OR email legal@robloxforge.app with subject "GDPR Erasure Request"
- Response deadline: 30 calendar days

**Exceptions (we will retain):**
- Payment logs (7 years, IRS requirement)
- Parental consent verification (5 years, FTC requirement)
- Legal disputes or DMCA claims (7 years)

### 5.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to request that we limit how we use your data (e.g., restrict to legal purposes only).

**How to exercise:**
- Email legal@robloxforge.app with subject "GDPR Restrict Processing Request"
- Specify which processing activities to restrict
- Response deadline: 15 calendar days

**Effect:**
- We will not use your data for the restricted purposes
- Data will be retained but not actively processed

### 5.5 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.

**How to exercise:**
- Email legal@robloxforge.app with subject "GDPR Data Portability Request"
- Response deadline: 15 calendar days

**What you'll receive:**
- Account data (email, name, DOB, profile)
- Project files and assets (in Roblox format)
- Transaction history
- All communications with us

### 5.6 Right to Object

You have the right to object to processing of your personal data.

**How to exercise:**
- Email legal@robloxforge.app with subject "GDPR Objection Request"
- Specify which processing activities you object to
- Response deadline: 15 calendar days

**We can continue processing if:**
- We have a compelling legitimate interest
- You need it to perform a contract
- It's required by law

### 5.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making

We do NOT use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or significant effects on you.

## 8. COOKIES AND TRACKING

### 8.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They allow us to:
- Remember you between visits
- Maintain your login session
- Understand how you use the platform
- Prevent fraud and abuse

### 8.2 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** (Required)
- Session cookies (login, authentication)
- CSRF tokens (security)
- Preference cookies (dark mode setting)
- Cannot be disabled; required for the Service to function

**Analytics Cookies** (Optional)
- PostHog analytics (feature usage, page views)
- You can opt-out anytime (see 8.3)

**Third-Party Cookies**
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Clerk (authentication)
- Sentry (error reporting)

### 8.3 Your Cookie Choices

**In your account settings:**
- Disable analytics cookies
- Opt-out of behavioral tracking
- Clear all stored cookies
- Download cookie usage report

**In your browser:**
- Disable all cookies (may break the Service)
- Block third-party cookies only
- Delete cookies on exit

**For California residents:**
Your browser's "Global Privacy Control" signal is respected:
- If enabled, we disable all optional cookies
- No additional action required

### 8.4 Pixel Tags and Web Beacons

We may use pixels (1×1 images) to:
- Track email opens (in notification emails)
- Measure ad campaign effectiveness
- Understand what content resonates

You can disable pixel tracking:
- Email legal@robloxforge.app to opt-out
- Most email clients block pixels by default

### 8.5 Do Not Track

Some browsers have "Do Not Track" (DNT) settings. We honor DNT signals by:
- Disabling all optional tracking cookies
- Not building behavioral profiles
- Limiting data retention to 30 days

## 9. DATA SECURITY

### 9.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard safeguards:

**Encryption**
- HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit (256-bit)
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Password hashing (bcrypt with salt)
- Payment data encrypted (PCI DSS Level 1)

**Access Control**
- Principle of least privilege (employees see only needed data)
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for staff
- Session timeouts (30 minutes of inactivity)

**Network Security**
- Web Application Firewall (WAF)
- DDoS protection
- Regular penetration testing
- Intrusion detection systems (IDS)

**Monitoring and Auditing**
- Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)
- Automated threat detection
- 24/7 SOC monitoring
- Audit logs (minimum 12 months retention)

**Secure Deletion**
- Data deleted via cryptographic shredding
- Overwrite with random data (DOD 5220.22-M standard)
- Verified deletion with checksums

**Personnel Security**
- Background checks (FCRA compliant)
- Confidentiality agreements
- Annual security training
- Zero-tolerance for unauthorized access

### 9.2 Third-Party Vendor Security

All vendors with access to data must:
- Maintain SOC 2 Type II certification (or equivalent)
- Pass annual third-party security audits
- Implement encryption, access controls, audit logs
- Report security incidents within 24 hours
- Comply with our Data Processing Agreement

### 9.3 What We Don't Guarantee

We implement reasonable security, but no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee:
- Absolute protection against unauthorized access
- Protection against state-sponsored attackers
- Protection against physical theft or coercion

If a breach occurs:
1. We will investigate within 24 hours
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours
3. Provide credit monitoring (if applicable)
4. Report to regulators (if required by law)

### 9.4 Your Security Responsibilities

To protect your account:
- Use a strong, unique password (12+ characters, mix of types)
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share your password or recovery codes
- Log out of shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Keep your email secure (email recovery option)
