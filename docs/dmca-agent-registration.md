**Priority:** HIGH (safe harbor requires registered agent per 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(2))

---

## Why This Is Required

To qualify for DMCA safe harbor protection (17 U.S.C. § 512), service providers must:

1. Register a designated copyright agent with the U.S. Copyright Office
2. Make agent contact information publicly available on the website

Without registration, the platform **cannot** claim DMCA safe harbor, exposing ForjeGames LLC to
full copyright liability for user-uploaded content.

---

## Step-by-Step: Form HAL Filing

### 1. Create an eCO Account

Go to: https://eco.copyright.gov
Create an account at the U.S. Copyright Office Electronic Copyright Office (eCO) portal.

**Account type:** Service Provider (not individual copyright registration)

---

### 2. Navigate to DMCA Agent Directory

After logging in:
- Go to: https://dmca.copyright.gov/osp/publish/home.htm
- This is the **DMCA Designated Agent Directory** (separate from general copyright registration)
- Click "Register a New Service Provider"

---

Fill in the following fields:

| **Full legal name of service provider** | ForjeGames LLC |
| **Alternative names (dba)** | ForjeGames |

| **Agent's full address** | [your address] |
| **Agent's phone number** | [your phone] |
| **Agent's email address** | dmca@forjegames.com |
| **Agent's website URL (for notices)** | https://forjegames.com/dmca |

**Service Provider URL:** https://forjegames.com

---

### 4. Pay the Filing Fee

- Fee: **$6.00 per designation** (as of 2024 — verify current fee at copyright.gov)
- Payment: Credit card via eCO portal
- Designations expire every **3 years** — set a calendar reminder to renew

---

### 5. Receive Confirmation

The Copyright Office will:
- Issue a confirmation email within 1-3 business days
- List your agent in the public DMCA Agent Directory
- Provide a registration ID

**Save the confirmation email and registration ID.** Store a copy in:
`docs/legal-registrations/dmca-agent-confirmation.pdf`

---

### 6. Publish Agent Info

Agent contact information is already published at:
- `https://forjegames.com/dmca` (the DMCA policy page)
- `https://forjegames.com/terms` (Section 10)

This satisfies the 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(2) public disclosure requirement.

## Ongoing Requirements

To maintain safe harbor, ForjeGames must also:

1. **Adopt and reasonably implement** a repeat-infringer policy (documented in Terms of Service §10 and DMCA Policy)
2. **Accommodate standard technical measures** used by copyright owners (e.g., Content ID-style systems if adopted)
3. **Respond to valid takedown notices** within a reasonable time (our SLA: 48 hours acknowledgment)
4. **Not receive financial benefit** from infringing activity while having the right and ability to control it
